Automation Explorer

Automation Explorer in UiPath is a feature designed to help users discover and explore automation opportunities within their processes. It provides insights and recommendations on tasks that can be automated, making it easier to identify potential areas for improvement and efficiency gains.

Studio Web

UiPath Studio Web enables users to build and test cross-platform automations across online business apps and services. Studio Web is the recommended tool for getting started with automation.

Process Mining

Process Mining in UiPath involves analyzing and visualizing how business processes are executed in reality, compared to how they are intended to be. It helps identify inefficiencies, deviations, and opportunities for automation by examining data from your business processes.

Automation Hub

UiPath Automation Hub is a collaborative platform designed to help organizations manage and scale their automation initiatives. It provides a centralized location for capturing, evaluating, and prioritizing automation ideas and projects
